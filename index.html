Chrystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of play.

There are four chrystals below. By clicking on a chrystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number that was generated. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value assigned to each chrystal is hidden from you until you click on a chrystal.

The value for each chrystal changes when you start a new game. Have fun playing the Chrystal Game!
